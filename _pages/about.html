I am a Ph.D. candidate working on Learning-based Motion Planning for Drones at Delft University of Technology, where I am supervised by Prof. Marija Popovic at Micro Air Vehicle Lab(MAVLab). Previously, I was a research intern working on automatic mechanical design of legged robots and perceptive humanoid locomotion at Shanghai AI Lab, collaborating with Dr. Jiangmiao Pang and Dr. Gang Chen. Before that, I obtained Master's degree in Robotics in 2023, working on Planning with Uncertainty under the supervision of Prof. Javier Alonso-Mora at Autonomous Multi-Robots Lab.

My current research interests focus on Motion planning in unknown dynamic environments, Active Perception, and application of Reinforcement Learning. I love building robots.
